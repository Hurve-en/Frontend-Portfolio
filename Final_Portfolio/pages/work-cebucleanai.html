CITA-CebuCleanAI

A mobile-first waste classification concept connecting AI, local response, and cleaner civic workflows.

Mobile + AI + IoT

System Concept

Cleaner waste handling through mobile recognition and routing.

CebuCleanAI imagines a flow where residents can classify waste quickly while local responders gain clearer visibility into patterns, hotspots, and next actions.

Flutter TensorFlow Lite PostGIS IoT
View Repository Back to Work

Problem

Waste sorting is hard to make practical at scale.

The challenge here is not only identifying waste correctly, but also turning that recognition into something useful for residents and local cleanup systems.

Approach

Pair recognition with local decision-making.

  • Put classification into a fast mobile interaction.
  • Connected outputs to maps, routing, and municipal visibility.
  • Shaped the concept around day-to-day usefulness, not novelty alone.

Outcome

A stronger bridge between resident action and local response.

The project frames AI as one part of a larger civic system, where data, geography, and responsiveness all matter.

Reflection

What I'd Improve Next

My next step would be to build out a more robust backend with stronger security and better infrastructure planning, so the app could support high traffic, protect user and location data more effectively, and scale for thousands of active users.
