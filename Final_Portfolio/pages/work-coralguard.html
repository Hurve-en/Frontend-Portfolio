CITA-CoralGuardAI

An AI-assisted reef monitoring experience designed to make local reporting more structured, readable, and useful.

AI + Mapping

Protecting Cebu's reefs through clearer community reporting.

CoralGuardAI turns underwater image submissions into structured reef health signals, helping communities, divers, and local stakeholders review information more quickly.

React Supabase Google Gemini Leaflet
View Repository Back to Work

Problem

Reef reporting often lacks usable structure.

The project focuses on making reef observations easier to submit and easier to understand, especially for people who need more than raw images and scattered notes.

Approach

AI, maps, and storage working together.

  • Turned image submissions into structured monitoring data.
  • Connected reports to mapping for clearer geographic context.
  • Built the experience around readable, actionable outputs.

Outcome

Designed to feel useful beyond the demo.

The result is a more grounded product concept, where interface, AI assistance, and data visibility all support the same reporting workflow.

Reflection

What I'd Improve Next

I would strengthen the backend architecture so the platform can handle much larger reporting volume, improve security around user data and access, and make the system more reliable for thousands of users interacting at the same time.
